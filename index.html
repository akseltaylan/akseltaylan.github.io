hello there! i'm aksel. i'm an engineer with a deep passion for computer graphics. currently, i'm the pipeline developer for texas a&m's visualization department. in the past, i was a software engineer and technical artist at the learning interactive visualization experience lab. in the summer of 2019, i'll be a technical director intern at blue sky studios!

i've always been drawn to the creative realm - my actions have been rooted in inspiring others through stunning visual imagery and powerful stories since i was a kid. as it happens, computer graphics is the perfect marriage of this and my love for technology and mathematics. i strive to combine my creative intuition and analytical skillset to empower artists in their quest to provoke emotion and feeling in their audience.

primary areas of interest: art-directable physically-based modeling and simulations, optimizations of rendering algorithms using deep learning, storytelling in virtual reality, lighting and shading
